MediCheck: AI-powered Symptom Checker and Diagnosis Tool

MediCheck is a sophisticated web-based application designed to provide users with quick and accurate diagnostic insights based on reported symptoms. Leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, MediCheck assists users in identifying potential health conditions by analyzing their symptoms.

Key Features:

  • Symptom Selection: Users can select their symptoms from a comprehensive list provided by the application.
  • Search Functionality: To facilitate ease of use, MediCheck includes a search bar that allows users to quickly find and select specific symptoms.
  • AI Diagnosis: Once the user submits their selected symptoms, MediCheck utilizes an AI-powered decision tree classifier to analyze the symptoms and predict potential health conditions.
  • Confidence Level: In addition to providing users with a predicted health condition, MediCheck also calculates a confidence level for the diagnosis.
