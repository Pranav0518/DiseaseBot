Basic Medication Tips for Cold

1. Rest and stay hydrated.

2. Use over-the-counter cold medications to relieve symptoms such as congestion, cough, and fever.

3. Gargle with warm salt water to soothe a sore throat.

4. Use saline nasal sprays or nasal irrigation to clear nasal passages.

5. Wash hands frequently and avoid close contact with sick individuals.
