Fever

Basic Medication Tips for Fever

1. Rest and drink plenty of fluids.

2. Take acetaminophen (Tylenol) or ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin) to reduce fever and relieve discomfort.

3. Use a cool compress or take a cool bath to lower body temperature.

4. Keep the environment cool and well-ventilated.

5. If fever persists or worsens, seek medical attention.
